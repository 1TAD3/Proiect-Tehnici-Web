World Records and Achievements

  • First artist to have over 2,000 songs on SoundCloud.
  • "Lucid Dreams" reached over 1 billion streams on Spotify.
  • His album *Legends Never Die* debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 with over 497,000 album-equivalent units sold in its first week.
  • Juice WRLD became the most streamed artist on Spotify in 2020.
  • Posthumously, he broke the record for the most tracks by a solo artist in the top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100 simultaneously.
